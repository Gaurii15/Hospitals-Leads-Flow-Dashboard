# 🏥 Hospitals-Leads-Flow-Dashboard
The **Papollo Hospitals Healthcare Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to monitor hospital operations, patient flow, diagnosis trends, billing performance, and bed occupancy. It enables hospital administrators and management teams to analyze key healthcare metrics for better operational planning.

![Dashboard Preview](Dashboard(3).png)


Hospitals generate large volumes of patient and operational data every day. Without proper visualization, identifying trends such as bed utilization, diagnosis distribution, billing performance, and doctor-wise workload becomes challenging.

This dashboard transforms raw hospital data into actionable insights, helping management to:

- Monitor hospital occupancy
- Track patient admissions and discharges
- Analyze diagnosis trends
- Compare insurance claims with billing amounts
- Evaluate doctor-wise patient feedback
- Improve resource allocation and operational efficiency

- Patient ID
- Date Range Slicer

## 📈 Key Insights

- Analyze hospital bed utilization across Private, General, and ICU wards.
- Identify the most common patient diagnoses.
- Compare healthcare billing with insurance claims.
- Measure doctor-wise patient engagement through feedback.
- Track revenue generated from different diagnostic tests.
